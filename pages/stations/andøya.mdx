# Andøya Weather Radar

Andøya weather radar is located on Trolltinden in Andøy municipality in Nordland county.

Andøya weather radar was installed in 2007. The radar tower is 5.5 meters high, and the radar antenna is 441 meters above sea level.

Andøya weather radar shows precipitation and wind for Vesterålen, parts of Troms and the sea areas outside.

On Andøya, a radar of the type SELEX Gematronik Meteor 500C is run

# Berlevåg Weather Radar

Berlevåg weather radar is located at the top Skužučohkka in the Gednje area in Berlevåg municipality, Finnmark county.

Berlevåg weather radar was installed in 2012. The radar tower is 17 meters high, and the radar antenna is 474 meters above sea level.

Berlevåg weather radar shows precipitation and wind for East Finnmark and the sea areas outside.

At Berlevåg, a radar of the type SELEX Gematronik Meteor 635CDP is operated.

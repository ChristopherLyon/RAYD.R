# Hasvik Weather Radar

Hasvik weather radar is located on Sluskfjellet in Hasvik municipality, Finnmark county.

Hasvik weather radar was installed in 2008. The radar tower is 6.5 meters high, and the radar antenna is 446.5 meters above sea level.

Hasvik weather radar shows precipitation and wind for West Finnmark and North Troms, as well as the sea areas outside.

At Hasvik, a radar of the type SELEX Gematronik Meteor 600C is operated.

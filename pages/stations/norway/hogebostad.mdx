# Hægebostad Weather Radar

Hægebostad weather radar is located on Stakksteinsliknuten in the Toreshei area in Hægebostad municipality in Agder county.

Hægebostad weather radar was installed in 2000. The radar tower is 7 meters high, and the radar antenna is 631 meters above sea level.

Hægebostad weather radar shows precipitation and wind for Sørlandet and Rogaland as well as parts of the Skagerrak.

At Hægebostad, a radar of the type METEOR 735CDP10 from Leonardo is operated.

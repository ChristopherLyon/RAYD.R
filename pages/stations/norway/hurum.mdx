# Hurum Weather Radar

Hurum weather radar is located on Nielsåsen in Asker municipality in Viken county.

Hurum weather radar was installed in 2010. The radar tower is 13.5 meters high, and the radar antenna is 362.7 meters above sea level.

Hurum weather radar shows precipitation and wind for the central Eastern Norway area, and is the weather radar closest to Oslo.

At Hurum, a radar of the type SELEX Gematronik Meteor 635CDP10 is operated.

# Rissa Weather Radar

Rissa weather radar is located on Nagelen in the area Olsøyheia in Indre Fosen municipality in Trøndelag county.

Rissa weather radar was installed in 2002. The radar tower is 9 meters high, and the radar antenna is 616 meters above sea level.

Rissa weather radar shows precipitation and wind for Trøndelag counties, parts of Møre- and Romsdal, and the sea areas Storegga, Frøyabanken and Haltenbanken.

On Rissa, a radar of the type METEOR 735CDP10 from Leonardo is operated.

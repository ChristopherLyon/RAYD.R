# Røst Weather Station

Røst weather radar is located on Langneset near the airport in Røst municipality in Nordland county.

Røst weather radar was installed in 2004. The radar tower is 12 meters high, and the radar antenna is 17 meters above sea level.

Røst weather radar shows precipitation and wind for Lofoten, Ofoten and Salten, as well as Røstbanken, the outer Vestfjord and parts of Trænabanken and Vesterålsbankene.

On Røst, a radar of the type SELEX Gematronik Meteor 500C is operated.
